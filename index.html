A Letter Under the Moon

My dearest Areen,

Tonight the sky belongs to you. Every star that blinked into existence did so only to witness how beautiful you are — and the crescent moon hangs there like a smile, the way I smile every time I think of you.

I have been searching for the right words all evening, but how do you describe the colour of something that makes the world feel warmer just by existing? You are that colour, Areen. You always have been.

This Chand Raat, I am not just wishing you a happy Eid. I am wishing that every day ahead feels as magical as tonight's sky — full of light, full of wonder, and full of a love that never dims.

Thank you for being the person I get to think of when I look up at the moon. There is no better gift this world could have given me.

Eid Mubarak, my love. Forever yours.

— Fahim 🌙
